What is Knowts?

Knowts is a note taking, planning and organisation app.

What can it do?

Knowts lets you oragnise groups of lists as Boards. Members with access to a board can be assigned items on a list. It's a simple, powerful way to organise.

How much does it cost?

Absolutely nothing! Just signup using the link at the top right to get started.
